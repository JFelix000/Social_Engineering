Types of Social Engineering

Social enginnering comes in many forms, some being more time and labor intensive than others.

Online Methods

  • Phishing

    • Perhaps the most common example of social engineering seen today is phising. With phishing scams, the individual trying to recieve information from their victum sends malicious content in the form of emails with links or attachmets. Such attacks typically cause the victum to feel a sense of urgency when or fear with the intent of making them act on a matter quickly. Such scams are accompished by impersonating a trusted figure and asking the victum for sensitive or personal information in order to have the issue resolved.

  • Baitting

    • Baiting is a form of social engineering in which the individual offers their victum a reward in exchange of information. These sites usually come in the form of sites that say the victum is the winner of a free gift. The user is then required to input personal information in order to claim their reward. From this hackers or identity theifs are able to gain access to private informations includign the victums address or credit card information.
